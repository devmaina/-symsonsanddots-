About Symsons & Dots Limited

Symsons and Dots Limited is a premier firm specializing in a comprehensive range of services including Building & Civil Engineering Contracting, Sales & Rental of Heavy Machinery, and the supply of essential Building Materials. Based in Nairobi, Kenya, we are dedicated to delivering excellence in every project.

Our expertise extends to large-scale operations such as Mass Excavation, efficient Backfilling, reliable Dumping services, and General Transport and Logistics. With a commitment to quality, safety, and client satisfaction, we leverage our extensive experience and state-of-the-art equipment to meet the diverse needs of the construction and infrastructure sectors.

We pride ourselves on our professional approach, timely execution, and the ability to provide tailored solutions that ensure the success of your ventures. Partner with Symsons and Dots Limited for reliable and robust solutions that build the future.

Our Equipment Rentals

Excavators

Powerful machines for digging, demolition, and heavy lifting.

Backhoes

Versatile equipment for digging, trenching, and loading.

Bulldozers

Robust machines for pushing earth, clearing land, and grading.

Graders

Used for creating flat surfaces, fine grading, and road construction.

Low Loaders

Specialized trailers for transporting heavy and oversized equipment.

Pavers

Machines designed for laying asphalt or concrete on roads.

Compactors

Industrial trucks used to lift and move materials over short distances.
